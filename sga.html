About Us

The mission of the Student Government Association of Stevens Institute of Technology (SGA) is to advocate for the undergraduate student body and serve as a means of communication and input between students, faculty, and staff. The SGAs imperative is enhancing the student experience and quality of life on campus while providing developmental opportunities for students. Charging itself with being a voice for the student body, the SGA is responsive to the issues, rights, and concerns of Stevens students. The SGA upholds a high standard of integrity and represents the university and student body with honor.
